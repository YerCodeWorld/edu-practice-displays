Meet the Family

Hello, young explorer! Every story has characters, and the first characters we meet in life are usually our family. A family is a group of people who are related to each other, like parents and their children. They share love, support, and many adventures together.

In this chapter, we'll learn the words for different family members and discover a special grammar trick to talk about the things that belong to them. Let's begin!

Showing Who Owns What

How do we say that a book belongs to the daughter? We can't just say "daughter book". That sounds a bit silly!

We use a little magic called the possessive 's'. We add an apostrophe (') and an 's' to the person's name to show something belongs to them.

This is the daughter's This shows the book belongs to the daughter. book.

Try hovering over the special word above! This little 's tells us who the owner is. Let's look at another one:

That is the father's This shows the car belongs to the father. car.
